Have you heard of reading well?

It is a prescription of books at local libraries for mental health issues

Check to see if your library has them, Barking and Dagenham Library definitely do. You can also find these books on the Amazon website, called the overcoming series. Topics include below I have placed as many as I can but there is always a condition yours perhaps that is not shown, just head over to Amazon. If you cannot get a copy at your local library they may order one in for free.
