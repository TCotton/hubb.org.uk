Debt and mental health

Debt worsens when mentally ill according to Money Expert - Martin Lewis of TV and Money Saving Expert has devloped a charity especially for the mentally ill in debt. Access here, the website is free to join and has a lot of free advice and some paid surveys. For instance he recommends five places for help with debt and not to the debt counsellors around such as Provident etc whom charge high interest as do moneylenders or loansharks.

Have you heard of reading well?

It is a prescription of books at local libraries for mental health issues

Check to see if your library has them, Barking and Dagenham Library definitely do. You can also find these books on the Amazon website, called the overcoming series. Topics include below I have placed as many as I can but there is always a condition yours perhaps that is not shown, just head over to Amazon. If you cannot get a copy at your local library they may order one in for free.

  • Anorexia
  • Low self-esteem
  • Weight issues

  • Depersonalisation
